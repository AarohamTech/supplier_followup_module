# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Procurement staff, managers, administrators, and supplier accounts use the product during operational purchasing workflows. Staff monitor purchase orders, prioritize exceptions, coordinate follow-ups, review communication, and track shipments. Suppliers respond to purchase-order requests and maintain shipment information through a focused portal.

## Product Purpose

Supplier Follow-up Agent is an industrial procurement control tower. It brings purchase-order status, supplier communication, follow-up automation, risk signals, and shipment tracking into one dependable workspace. Success means users can identify what needs attention, understand why, and take the correct action quickly without losing context.

## Brand Personality

Industrial, professional, and minimal. The interface should feel precise, calm, dependable, and operational rather than decorative or promotional.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy SaaS styling, oversized marketing treatments, excessive rounding, ornamental gradients, glass effects, decorative card grids, and gratuitous animation. Avoid interfaces that hide operational density or urgency behind visual novelty.

## Design Principles

1. Make priority and next action obvious.
2. Preserve useful operational density while maintaining clear hierarchy.
3. Use restrained brand color to communicate meaning, not decoration.
4. Keep staff and supplier experiences cohesive, predictable, and task-focused.
5. Prefer quiet, immediate feedback over attention-seeking motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction standards. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, reduced motion, readable responsive layouts, and status communication that never depends on color alone.
